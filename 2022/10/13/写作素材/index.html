Benefits of Advertising

  1. Out of commercial purposes, advertising makes products more attractive to consumers.

  7. Advertising can create jobs because company may prompt expansion.

  9. Advertising, a critical information source, provides people with a visual experience.

  10. Advertising can help people release pressure and obtain relaxation and recreation by generating purchasing desire.

  11. Informative and educational advertisements can stimulate children’s cognitive development and expand the scope of knowledge in children.

  12. Those commercials introducing healthy dietary habits can help children form good habits.

  13. Some advertisements let children know more about the world and enable them to expand their horizons.

  14. Advertising can make children view the world from different perspectives.

Drawbacks of Advertising

  1. The commercials tempt people into following the latest trend and prompt them to make irrational decisions.

  3. Advertising gives consumers a false perception of the product because some advertisers exaggerate the effects of their products.
  4. Advertisements lead to the prevalence of consumerism and materialism, causing people to feel dissatisfied with their possessions and who they are.
  5. Some advertisements promoting unhealth food, such as junk food, high-fat and high-sugar foods, lead to obesity and diabetes.
  6. Commercials are partially responsible for deteriorating moral and ethical values because they prompt smoking, excessive drinking, and a hedonistic lifestyle.
  7. Advertising not only manipulates people into spending money on products beyond their purchase power but entices them to buy more than they need.
  8. Large-scale competitive advertising will trigger an advertising war, leading to the increasing cost of products and services.
  9. Advertising can lead to unequal competition because small companies cannot match the same advertising budgets as large companies.
  10. Advertising can also create a monopolistic market because it drives small businesses out of the market.
  11. Advertising leads to excessive consumption and a throw-away society, causing resource strain.
  12. Advertising may create more unnecessary waste for our society, and this waste will do damage to our environment because it leads to excessive consumption.
  13. Commercials also make children have cognitive errors and develop a comparing mentality because they cannot view marketing messages critically.
  14. Consumers feel deceived when they receive products that are not good as advertisements’ announcements.
  15. Commercials make people feel less confident and dissatisfied with their current lives because advertisements tempt people to follow the latest trends.
  16. Some advertisements do not consider children so youngsters may pick up unhealthy habits and lifestyles, such as smoking and excessive drinking.

Measures to Protect People from the Power of Advertising

  1. The authority should make laws to restrict advertisements aimed at kids.
  2. Parents should explain to children the tricks behind the commercials and help them view information critically.
  3. Parents can set electronic devices into a kid-friendly mode, preventing them from seeing inappropriate content.
